Game Rules

Everything you need to play is explained below!

How to Play

  • Starting the Game:
    • Choose your token.
    • Each player rolls the dice to determine their starting yellow space.
    • After placement, yellow spaces become Wild Card Spaces.
  • Movement:
    • Roll the dice and move your piece forward the corresponding number of spaces.
    • The space you land on determines your question category.
  • Answering Questions:
    • Draw a card matching the category of your space.
    • Start the timer when the question is revealed (30 seconds).
    • Choose your difficulty:
      • Easy: Worth 1 point.
      • Hard: Worth 3 points.
  • Earning Points:
    • Answer correctly within 30 seconds to earn points and keep the card.
    • If you fail to answer, earn no points, and your turn ends.
  • Special Spaces:
    • Bonus/Penalty Spaces: Draw a card that can either boost your progress or waste other players’ time.
    • Challenge Spaces:
      • Another player picks a category for you to answer on yellow spaces or when on the same space as an opponent.
      • If you answer correctly, earn a special card with unique effects to use on your turn.
      • If you fail, your opponent earns the special card instead.

Materials

  • Game board with spaces corresponding to categories (Cybersecurity, Fake News, Internet History, SEO, UX).
  • Deck of category cards (one deck per category).
  • Dice for movement.
  • Player pieces.
  • At least one smartphone with a QR code scanner.

Winning the Game

  • Be the first player to collect at least 5 points in each of the five categories:
    • Cybersecurity
    • Fake News
    • Internet History
    • SEO
    • UX
  • Complete a mini-game in each category to validate your skills.
  • Once all categories are mastered, the player becomes the Master of the Web and the wise of the Internet!
